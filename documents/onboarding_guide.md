# Trenkwalder Group - Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Trenkwalder!

Congratulations on joining the Trenkwalder Group — one of the leading HR service providers in Central and Eastern Europe. With more than 200 locations in 16 countries, around 16,000 employees, and a turnover of 600 million euros, we are proud of our 40+ years of continuous growth and innovation.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to fulfill the European promise of free, cross-border work. We focus on qualified professionals — the backbone of the European economy — and on the consistent integration of modern technologies.

## Your First Week

### Day 1: Setup & Orientation
- Collect your hardware (laptop, monitors, peripherals) from IT
- Set up your development environment
- Access credentials will be sent to your company email
- Meet your team lead and buddy

### Day 2-3: Company Introduction
- Attend the company culture presentation
- Review the employee handbook
- Complete mandatory compliance training
- Set up your project management tools (Jira, Confluence)

### Day 4-5: Team Integration
- Join your first sprint planning or standup
- Review current project documentation
- Pair programming session with your buddy
- One-on-one with your direct manager

## Development Standards

- All code must be reviewed via pull requests
- Follow the team's coding style guide
- Write unit tests for all new features (minimum 80% coverage)
- Use semantic commit messages
- Document all architectural decisions in ADRs

## Work Arrangements

### Remote Work Policy
Employees can work remotely up to 4 days per week. At least 1 day per week should be spent in the office or at a co-working space for team collaboration. Remote work arrangements must be agreed upon with your direct manager.

### Core Hours
Core working hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM CET. Outside of core hours, you can flexibly arrange your working time between 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM.

### Equipment
The company provides:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or ThinkPad, your choice)
- External monitor
- Keyboard and mouse
- Noise-cancelling headphones
- Home office budget of €500 per year

## Communication

- **Urgent matters**: Slack direct message or phone call
- **Non-urgent**: Slack channel or email
- **Meeting-free blocks**: Wednesday and Friday afternoons are meeting-free for focused work
- **All-hands**: Monthly company-wide meeting, first Monday of each month

## Career Development

- Annual performance review with your manager
- Personal development budget of €2,000 per year for courses, conferences, and certifications
- Internal knowledge-sharing sessions every two weeks
- Mentoring program available for all levels
