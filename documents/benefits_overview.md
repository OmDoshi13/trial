# Trenkwalder Benefits Overview

## Employee Benefits Package 2025-2026

### Health & Wellness

**Supplementary Health Insurance**
All full-time employees are enrolled in the Trenkwalder supplementary health insurance plan at no additional cost. The plan covers:
- Dental care: Up to €1,500 per year for preventive and restorative treatments
- Vision care: Up to €400 per year for eye exams and corrective lenses
- Preventive health screenings: Annual comprehensive health check-up fully covered
- Mental health support: 12 sessions per year with licensed therapists, fully covered

**Gym & Fitness Allowance**
Employees receive a monthly fitness allowance of €50, reimbursable against gym memberships, fitness classes, or sports equipment purchases.

**Ergonomic Workplace**
In addition to the €500 annual home office budget, employees can request ergonomic workplace assessments. The company covers the cost of recommended ergonomic equipment for both office and home workstations.

### Financial Benefits

**Pension Scheme**
- Company contribution: 3% of gross monthly salary
- Voluntary employee contribution: Up to 4% additional (tax-deductible)
- Vesting period: 2 years
- Managed by Allianz Pension Fund

**Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP)**
- Available to employees with 1+ year of tenure
- Purchase Trenkwalder shares at 15% discount
- Maximum annual contribution: €5,000
- Quarterly purchase windows

**Referral Bonus**
- €2,000 for successful referral of a technical hire
- €1,000 for non-technical positions
- Paid after referred employee completes 6 months

### Learning & Development

**Personal Development Budget**
- €2,000 per year per employee
- Can be used for: online courses, certifications, conferences, books, workshops
- Must be agreed with manager during annual development planning
- Unused budget does not carry over

**Internal Learning**
- Bi-weekly knowledge sharing sessions (Tech Talks)
- Annual hackathon (2-day event)
- Mentoring program (available at all levels)
- Access to O'Reilly Learning, Udemy Business, and Coursera for Business

**Certification Support**
The company fully covers the cost of job-relevant certifications including:
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect
- Google Cloud Professional
- Kubernetes Administrator (CKA)
- Scrum Master / Product Owner certifications

### Time Off & Leave

**Annual Leave**
- 25 vacation days per calendar year
- Up to 5 days can be carried over (must be used by March 31)
- Pro-rated for part-time employees

**Special Leave**
- Marriage: 2 days
- Birth of child: 3 days
- Bereavement (immediate family): 3 days
- Moving: 1 day
- Blood donation: Half day

**Sabbatical**
Employees with 5+ years of tenure can request an unpaid sabbatical of up to 3 months. Position and role are guaranteed upon return.

### Other Perks

**Company Events**
- Annual summer party
- Christmas celebration
- Quarterly team events (budget: €50 per person)
- Monthly virtual coffee lottery (random 1:1 coffee chats across teams)

**Mobility**
- Public transit subsidy: €50/month
- Bike leasing program through JobRad
- Free parking at office locations

**Family**
- 2 weeks fully paid paternity leave
- Flexible return-to-work arrangements for new parents
- Emergency childcare support service
